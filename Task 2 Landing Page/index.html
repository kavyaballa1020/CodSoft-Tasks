Our Courses

B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering

Embark on an exciting journey in the world of computer science and engineering. Explore cutting-edge technologies, develop software solutions, and master the art of programming. Our comprehensive curriculum covers topics such as algorithms, data structures, software engineering, computer networks, and emerging fields like artificial intelligence and machine learning.

B.Sc in Agriculture

Embark on a journey into the field of agriculture. Learn about sustainable farming practices, crop management, agricultural economics, and more. Our program combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience to prepare you for a rewarding career in agriculture.

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering

Unleash your creativity and problem-solving skills in the field of mechanical engineering. Design and develop innovative mechanical systems, products, and manufacturing processes. Our program covers topics such as thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, materials science, computer-aided design (CAD), and sustainable engineering practices.
